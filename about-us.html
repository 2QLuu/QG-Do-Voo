introducing

QG do Voo Livre is a paragliding club located in Brazil. The club is dedicated to promoting the sport of paragliding and providing its members with the resources and training they need to safely and enjoyably participate in the activity. Whether you are a seasoned pro or a complete beginner, QG do Voo Livre has something to offer. From regular club meetings and training sessions to organized flights and competitions, the club provides a supportive and inclusive community for all those interested in the thrilling sport of paragliding.


Events and more

  • Organizing and hosting paragliding competitions and events.

  • Training and certifying members to become safe and proficient pilots.

  • Setting and achieving flight distance and duration records.

  • Participating in conservation and land-use advocacy efforts.

  • Helping to grow the sport of paragliding by introducing new people to the activity.

  • Hosting social events and gatherings for members to connect and share their passion for the sport.
